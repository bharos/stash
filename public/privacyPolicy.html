You are responsible for ensuring that your posts do not violate any confidentiality agreements you have signed. We do not encourage users to share proprietary or confidential information.

If we receive a valid complaint regarding a post violating confidentiality agreements, we may remove the content. Repeated violations may result in account suspension.

To report a violation, contact stash.team.contact@gmail.com.

4. Notifications and Email Preferences

Stash provides various types of notifications about activity related to your content, including comments, replies, and likes. By default, these notifications may be sent as follows:

  • In-app Notifications: All notifications appear in your notifications center within the Stash platform.
  • Email Digests: You may receive email digests (daily or weekly) containing a summary of notifications and activity.

You can customize your notification preferences at any time through your account settings. Options include:

  • Receiving immediate email notifications
  • Receiving daily digest emails
  • Receiving weekly digest emails
  • Opting out of email notifications entirely

We respect your communication preferences and will only send emails according to your selected preferences. You can unsubscribe from email notifications at any time.
